HOW WE STARTED

Founded in 2015, LensCraft began as a small group of friends exploring the outskirts of Washington DC with nothing but old film cameras. Today, we are a community of over 500 students.

We believe that photography isn't just about expensive gear; it's about the eye behind the viewfinder. Our journey started in a garage and has grown into a premier academy.

OUR MISSION

"To empower creative souls to capture the world's beauty through technical mastery and artistic freedom."
